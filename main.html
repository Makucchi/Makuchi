ABOUT ME

Hello! I am Makuchi, I'm a self-taught artist. I draw 2d girls from anime and from games that has cute anime girls like Genshin Impact, Nikke, Honkai Impact, and some other games that I find interesting. I draw traditionaly before, around 2017 to 2019. I draw human portraits and I copy anime pictures from google, but I got tired of it becuase I find that copying does not feel like I'm a real artist, that's why around year 2020 I bought my first drawing tablet (Huion hs64) and I started learning some basic female anatomy so I can create my own pose and so I can draw fanart of characters that I like. And what you see now is what I've become. Still not great but I'm happy on what I achieved ♥
